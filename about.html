Our Story

M.B YARN SERVICES. started as a small local business supplying yarn to local knitters and crocheters. The founder, a passionate crafter and entrepreneur, saw a gap in the market for high-quality, ethically sourced yarns and set out to fill it. In the early days, We sourced yarns from local farmers and spinners, building relationships with them to ensure that their yarns were of the highest quality. Today, We are a leading player in the global yarn market, with a loyal customer base and partnerships with some of the biggest names in the crafting industry. The company’s success is a testament to its commitment to excellence and its dedication to promoting the joys of crafting and creativity.
