Socks Sector

We provides yarn commonly used in the sock sector is a blend of wool and nylon. The count of this type of yarn varies from 10 to 20, with the most common count being 16 which is specifically designed for use in the socks sector. These yarns are durable, soft, and comfortable, making them ideal for use in the production of high-quality socks.

knitting Sector

We provides yarns for use in the knitting sector , knitting sector is worsted weight yarn. The count of this type of yarn is usually 4 ply or medium weight, with a gauge of around 20 stitches and 24 rows per 4 inches.. These yarns are versatile, strong, making them ideal for use in the production of a wide range of knitted products.
