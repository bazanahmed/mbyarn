Services

The Company deals in a variety of yarn types, including knitting, weaving, carded and combed auto coro, open end, HTR grey, and mealanges quickly

Socks Sector

We provides yarn commonly used in the sock sector is a blend of cotton and polyester. The count of this type of yarn varies from 10 to 20, with the most common count being 16 which is specifically designed for use in the socks sector. These yarns are durable, soft, and comfortable, making them ideal for use in the production of high-quality socks.

knitting Sector

We provides yarns for use in the knitting sector , knitting sector is worsted weight yarn. The count of this type of yarn is usually 4 ply or medium weight, with a gauge of around 20 stitches and 24 rows per 4 inches.. These yarns are versatile, strong, making them ideal for use in the production of a wide range of knitted products.

Weaving Sector

We provides yarns for use in the weaving sector. These yarns are strong, durable, and easy to work with, making them ideal for use in the production of a wide range of woven products, . the count for this is 10/2 silk yarn means that the yarn is made up of 10 strands, each of which is 2-ply and fine in thickness.

Ring Spun Yarn

We provides yarns of specific length of yarn, usually 840 yards, and calculating the number of yards that would weigh one pound & Commonly used counts for ring spun yarns range from 10s to 120s, with lower counts indicating thicker yarns and higher counts indicating finer yarns.

PC/CVC

We provides PC/CVC yarn which is widely used in the textile industry for making a variety of products such as t-shirts, jeans, workwear, and home textiles , Generally, a blend of 60% cotton and 40% polyester is used. This ensures the yarn has a soft feel and good durability from polyester.

Cotton Yarn

We provides high-quality cotton yarn services for a wide range of industries. We specialize in the production and supply of premium quality cotton yarn that meets the specific requirements of our clients. Our team of experts is dedicated to providing the best quality yarn products.
